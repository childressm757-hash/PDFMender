ABOUT

What PDFMender Is

PDFMender is a court filing compliance utility. It’s designed to reduce common technical rejection causes in CM/ECF and state eFile systems—before submission.

What we do

  • Detect high-risk PDF features (interactive forms, XFA, encryption, JavaScript, attachments)
  • Convert documents into static, filing-ready PDFs where possible
  • Provide clerk-language reporting so your staff knows what changed

Technical processing only. No legal advice. Subject to clerk review.
