About PDFMender

PDFMender exists to solve a narrow and common problem: court filing rejections caused by interactive or unflattened PDF documents.

Many courts require PDFs to be fully static before submission. Documents created from word processors, form templates, or certain PDF tools often retain interactive elements that trigger rejection.

PDFMender removes these interactive components and produces a court-acceptable PDF suitable for same-day resubmission.

The tool is used by attorneys and legal staff to address filing issues efficiently and without unnecessary complexity.
