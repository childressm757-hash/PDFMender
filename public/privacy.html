Privacy Policy

PDFMender is designed to process documents automatically for the purpose of fixing court-related PDF filing issues.

Files uploaded to PDFMender are processed automatically and are deleted after processing. Files are not reviewed by humans and are not retained for any purpose beyond completing the requested operation.

PDFMender does not sell, share, or reuse uploaded documents or their contents.

Basic, non-identifying usage data may be collected to understand site performance and reliability.
