FAQ

Why are interactive PDFs rejected by courts?

Many filing systems block interactive/XFA PDFs because dynamic form content can fail automated processing. Courts typically require static PDFs.

What is an XFA form?

XFA is a dynamic PDF form technology. Many court portals do not accept XFA and may reject filings until converted to a static PDF.

How do I flatten a PDF for court filing?

Flattening converts interactive fields to static page content. PDFMender produces static output designed to reduce common rejection triggers.

Why are encrypted PDFs rejected?

Encryption/restrictions can block processing and scanning. Removing encryption improves compatibility with court filing portals.
